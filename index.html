Research Profile

I am an Associate Researcher at the University of Girona, working on data-driven and generative models for personalized healthcare, with a primary focus on Type 1 Diabetes. My research centers on digital twins, deep generative models, and physiological simulation to support prediction, personalization, and clinical decision-making.

Research Interests

  • Digital twins for diabetes and metabolic health
  • Generative deep learning (VAE, GAN, diffusion, transformers)
  • Physiological time-series modeling and simulation
  • HRV–CGM integration for hypoglycemia prediction
  • Synthetic data generation for healthcare AI
