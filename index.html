About

I am an Associate Researcher at the University of Girona (MICELAB). I lead research activities on the Personal Digital Twin initiative and contribute to translational work toward real-world deployment. My PhD focused on data-driven generative models for Type 1 Diabetes simulation (Cum Laude).

Education

  • PhD, University of Girona (2020–2023) — Data-driven models for T1D using generative deep learning (Cum Laude)
  • MSc Electrical Engineering, CECOS University (2014–2017)
  • BSc Electrical Engineering, CECOS University (2009–2013)

Current Focus

  • Digital twins for therapy adjustment in diabetes
  • Generative simulation of glucose dynamics
  • Synthetic data for improving clinical ML models
  • RAG-driven tooling for research and deployment workflows

Research

  • Data-based digital twins for diabetes and metabolic health
  • Generative models for physiological time series (conditional synthesis, plausibility constraints)
  • Hypoglycemia detection/prediction; HRV–CGM integration
  • Synthetic data augmentation for robust clinical AI
