About

I am an Associate Researcher at the University of Girona. I lead research activities on the Personal Digital Twin initiative and contribute to grant applications, academic supervision, and translational work toward real-world deployment. My PhD focused on data-driven generative models for Type 1 Diabetes simulation (Cum Laude).
